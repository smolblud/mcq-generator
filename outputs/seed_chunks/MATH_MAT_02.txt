Row operations produce equivalent systems. Swap rows: R1 <-> R2. Example: [[4 8 3],[2 4 5],[7 1 2]] with R1<->R2 becomes [[2 4 5],[4 8 3],[7 1 2]]. This interchanges the corresponding equations.
