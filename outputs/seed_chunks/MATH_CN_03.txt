Example: simplify sqrt(-18). Write sqrt(-18) = i sqrt(18). Since 18 = 9*2, sqrt(18)=sqrt(9)*sqrt(2)=3 sqrt(2). Thus sqrt(-18) = i * 3 sqrt(2) = 3 i sqrt(2).
This uses the property sqrt(ab)=sqrt(a)sqrt(b) for a,b>=0 and the identity sqrt(-a)=i sqrt(a).
